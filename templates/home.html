About Our Pricing System

MetalWorks Price Management System is designed for metal distributors, manufacturers, and suppliers who need to manage complex pricing relationships between raw materials and finished products.

Our smart pricing algorithm automatically updates all your product prices when raw material costs change, saving you hours of manual calculations and ensuring your pricing stays competitive and profitable.

Key Features:

  • Automatic price recalculation based on raw material costs
  • Customizable price coefficients for different product types
  • Multi-category product organization
  • Real-time price updates across all products
  • Secure admin authentication and access control
  • Price history tracking and audit logs
  • Responsive design for mobile and desktop
  • Easy integration with existing systems

How It Works

Step 1: Add Raw Materials
Enter your raw materials (Steel, Aluminum, Copper, etc.) with their current market prices.

Step 2: Create Products
Add your products and link them to raw materials. Set a base price and coefficient (how much the material cost affects the product).

Step 3: Update Material Prices
When material costs change, update the price once. Our algorithm automatically recalculates all linked product prices.

Example:
Steel costs $100/ton. You make Steel Pipes with base price $150 and coefficient 0.8 (80% pass-through).
If Steel increases to $120 (20% increase), Steel Pipe automatically updates to $174 ($150 + $150 √ó 0.8 √ó 0.20).
